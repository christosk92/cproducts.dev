## What this is

cproducts is the umbrella for a couple of products I'm building from Utrecht. Right now that means **Wavee**, an open-source Spotify desktop client for Windows, and **LOL Review**, an asynchronous coaching marketplace for League of Legends players.

This site exists for two reasons. First, to give each product a stable home with a real introduction — most of what's been written about Wavee lives in a README, and LOL Review's marketing site is rightly focused on selling reviews, not on telling the story behind it. Second, because I want a place to write occasionally about the bits of the work I find interesting: clean-room protocol implementations, async-first product design, weird edges of the Windows App SDK.

## What's next

Wavee is in beta and gets weekly improvements. LOL Review is live and shipping reviews to real players. I'll post when something is worth posting — not for the algorithm.

If you want to talk about either, [hello@cproducts.dev](mailto:hello@cproducts.dev).
