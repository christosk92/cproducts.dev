## What this is

cproducts is basically the place where I put the stuff I'm building from Utrecht.

Right now that means **Wavee**, an open-source Spotify desktop client for Windows that is still in dev, and **LOL Review**, a League of Legends review tool that is archived and offline for now.

I wanted one place that is not just a README, a launch page, or some random social post. Wavee mostly lives in code and screenshots right now. LOL Review had a site, but that domain is revoked for the moment, and the old site was made to sell reviews, not really explain the project.

So yeh, this is the boring answer: I just wanted a proper home for the products, and a place to write down the parts of the work that are actually interesting. Protocol stuff, weird Windows SDK issues, async product ideas, decisions that made sense at the time, all of that.

## What's next

Wavee is still in development. LOL Review is archived after processing real orders. I want to bring it back later, but not right now.

I'll write when something is worth writing down. Not on a schedule, not for SEO, just when there is something useful to say.
